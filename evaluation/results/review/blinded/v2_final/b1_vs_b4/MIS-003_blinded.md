## PERIOPERATIVE MORBIDITY OUTCOMES

### Blood Loss

**Meta-Analysis Summary:**
- MIS-TLIF reduced intraoperative blood loss by average of **206.10 mL** compared to open TLIF [pubmed_38476911]

**Comparative Studies:**
- Single-level spondylolisthesis: MIS-TLIF **256.3±139 mL** vs mini-open-TLIF **550.3±186 mL** (p=0.023) [pubmed_39264454]
- Multi-segment comparison: MIS-TLIF **126.1±150.1 mL** postoperative drainage vs Open-TLIF **332.0±178.5 mL** (p<0.001) [pubmed_38378729]

**Systematic Review Finding:**
- MIS demonstrated **40% reduction in postoperative blood loss** compared to open surgery in spinal infection cases [pubmed_40585707]

---

### Hospital Length of Stay (LOS)

**Meta-Analysis Summary:**
- MIS-TLIF shortened hospitalization time by **3.46 days** compared to open TLIF [pubmed_38476911]

**Comparative Studies:**
- Multi-segment disease: MIS-TLIF **6.4±2.0 days** vs Open-TLIF significantly higher (p<0.001) [pubmed_38378729]
- Single-level spondylolisthesis: MIS-TLIF **3.2±0.9 days** vs mini-open-TLIF **6.7±1.6 days** (p<0.001) [pubmed_39264454]
- Spinal infection cases: MIS demonstrated **50% shorter hospital stay** compared to open surgery [pubmed_40585707]

---

### Postoperative Bed Rest Time

- MIS-TLIF shortened postoperative bed rest time by **3.13 days** compared to open TLIF [pubmed_38476911]

---

### Operative Time

**Disadvantage for MIS-TLIF:**
- Multi-segment disease: MIS-TLIF **212.4±56.2 minutes** vs Open-TLIF **154.7±41.1 minutes** (p<0.001; difference of **57.7 minutes**) [pubmed_38378729]

---

### Infection Rates

- Umbrella review found MIS-TLIF had **reduced infection rates** compared to open approaches [pubmed_38245811]
- Specific rates not numerically detailed in the abstract provided

---

### Radiation Exposure (MIS Disadvantage)

- Single-level surgery: MIS-TLIF **84.4±17.2 seconds** fluoroscopy time vs mini-open-TLIF **29.9±5.4 seconds** (p<0.001; **2.8-fold increase**) due to percutaneous screw insertion [pubmed_39264454]
- Trade-off identified: increased radiation exposure with MIS approaches [pubmed_38245811]

---

## PAIN OUTCOMES

### Postoperative Leg Pain (VAS Score)

**Short-term:**
- Meta-analysis: MIS-TLIF VAS scores were **0.20 points lower** than open TLIF (p<0.05) [pubmed_38476911]
- Endoscopic TLIF: lower VAS for back pain at **postoperative day 7** (p<0.05) [pubmed_38480550]

**Long-term (1-2 years):**
- No significant difference in pain outcomes at **1 year and 2 years postoperatively** between endoscopic and open TLIF (p>0.05) [pubmed_38480550]

---

## FUNCTIONAL OUTCOMES & FUSION RATES

### Functional Recovery (ODI)

**Short-term:**
- Endoscopic TLIF showed lower ODI at **postoperative 1 month** (p<0.05) compared to open TLIF [pubmed_38480550]

**Long-term:**
- No significant difference at **1 year and 2 years** postoperatively [pubmed_38480550]

### Fusion Rates

**Long-term Fusion Achievement:**
- Network meta-analysis comparing MIS-TLIF, FE-TLIF, and BE-TLIF: **Long-term functional improvement and fusion rates were comparable across all three surgical approaches** [pubmed_41420307]
- This indicates MIS-TLIF achieves equivalent fusion rates to open TLIF at long-term follow-up

---

## COST-EFFECTIVENESS

**1-Year Analysis:**
- MIS substantially more favorable: **$42,635/QALY** vs Open **$226,304/QALY** (MIS approximately **5.3-fold more cost-effective**) [pubmed_39805471]

**2-Year Analysis:**
- Similar cost-effectiveness between MIS and open approaches [pubmed_39805471]

**QALY Gains:**
- TLIF demonstrated highest QALY gain at 1-year: **0.43** [pubmed_39805471]

---

## SUMMARY OF KEY FINDINGS

### MIS-TLIF Advantages:
1. **Blood loss reduction:** 206.10 mL less (206.10 mL absolute; 40% relative) [pubmed_38476911, pubmed_40585707]
2. **Shorter hospitalization:** 3.46 days reduction [pubmed_38476911]
3. **Reduced bed rest time:** 3.13 days shorter [pubmed_38476911]
4. **Lower infection rates** [pubmed_38245811]
5. **Superior short-term pain relief** (0.20 VAS points lower) and functional recovery [pubmed_38476911, pubmed_38480550]
6. **Significantly better 1-year cost-effectiveness:** $42,635 vs $226,304/QALY [pubmed_39805471]

### MIS-TLIF Disadvantages:
1. **Longer operative time:** 57.7 minutes longer for multi-segment disease (p<0.001) [pubmed_38378729]
2. **Increased radiation exposure:** 2.8-fold increase in fluoroscopy time (84.4 vs 29.9 seconds) [pubmed_39264454]

### Equivalent Outcomes:
1. **Long-term fusion rates:** Comparable to open TLIF [pubmed_41420307]
2. **Long-term clinical outcomes:** No significant difference at 1-2 years postoperatively [pubmed_38480550]
3. **2-year cost-effectiveness:** Similar between approaches [pubmed_39805471]

---

## Evidence Gaps

- Specific infection rate percentages not provided in abstracts
- Long-term follow-up (>2 years) data limited
- Comparison primarily focused on degenerative disease; limited data for trauma, tumor, or infection pathologies
- Heterogeneity in study designs and reporting methods acknowledged in cost-utility review [pubmed_39805471]
