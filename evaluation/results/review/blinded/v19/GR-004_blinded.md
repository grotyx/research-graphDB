## Primary Intervention Comparison: CDR vs ACDF

The most extensively studied comparison is between **Cervical Disc Arthroplasty/Replacement and Anterior Cervical Discectomy and Fusion (ACDF)**, with multiple systematic reviews and meta-analyses [pubmed_31863642, pubmed_40797275, pubmed_29753897, pubmed_38843960, pubmed_35113188, pubmed_29595628, pubmed_31431880, pubmed_40882232, pubmed_30062947] (Evidence level: 1a).

## Shared Outcome Measures

### Functional Outcomes
- **Neck Disability Index (NDI)**: Most commonly reported measure across all studies
- **Visual Analog Scale (VAS)**: Both neck pain and arm pain assessments
- **Japanese Orthopaedic Association (JOA) Score**: For neurological function [pubmed_31431880]
- **SF-36 Physical Component Score**: Quality of life assessment [pubmed_31863642, pubmed_30062947]

### Clinical Outcomes
- **Neurological Success Rates**: Standardized improvement thresholds [pubmed_40797275, pubmed_35113188]
- **Range of Motion**: Cervical spine mobility preservation [pubmed_40797275, pubmed_29753897, pubmed_31431880]
- **Adjacent Segment Disease**: Long-term degenerative changes [pubmed_29753897, pubmed_31431880, pubmed_30062947]

### Safety Outcomes
- **Complication Rates**: Surgical and device-related complications [pubmed_29753897]
- **Reoperation Rates**: Secondary surgical interventions [pubmed_29753897, pubmed_30062947]
- **Hospital Length of Stay**: Perioperative outcomes [pubmed_29753897]

## Evidence Gaps

The provided papers focus exclusively on cervical spine interventions comparing CDR variants with ACDF. **No lumbar spine surgery interventions** are represented despite the knowledge graph context mentioning lumbar radiculopathy treatment. Additionally, **no studies compare CDR with posterior cervical approaches** or other anterior decompression techniques beyond ACDF.

## Patient Selection Considerations

One meta-analysis noted that **myelopathic patients showed different outcome patterns** compared to radiculopathic patients, with myelopathic cohorts favoring ACDF while radiculopathic patients showed more favorable CDA outcomes [pubmed_40882232] (Evidence level: 1a).

The surgical site infection risk calculator [pubmed_41490806] (Evidence level: 2b) represents a different outcome domain entirely, focusing on perioperative complications rather than functional outcomes.

# Interventions Sharing Most Outcome Measures with Cervical Disc Replacement (CDR)

Based on the provided papers, **Anterior Cervical Discectomy and Fusion (ACDF)** and **Cervical Disc Arthroplasty (CDA)** are the interventions that share the most outcome measures with CDR across the literature.

## Shared Outcome Domains

### 1. **Functional/Pain Outcomes**
- NDI (Neck Disability Index) [pubmed_29753897, pubmed_31863642, pubmed_30062947]
- VAS (Visual Analog Scale) - neck and upper limb pain [pubmed_31431880, pubmed_30062947]
- Neurological success rates [pubmed_40797275]

### 2. **Motion Preservation**
- Range of motion at operated segment [pubmed_40797275, pubmed_31431880, pubmed_38843960]
- C2-C7 range of motion measurements [pubmed_31431880]

### 3. **Adjacent Segment Outcomes**
- Adjacent segment disease (ASD) [pubmed_31863642, pubmed_29753897, pubmed_30062947, pubmed_38843960]
- Adjacent level surgery rates [pubmed_31863642]
- Adjacent segment degeneration risk [pubmed_38843960]

### 4. **Secondary Surgery Rates**
- Total secondary surgery [pubmed_31863642, pubmed_29753897]
- Secondary surgery at index level [pubmed_31863642]
- Reoperation rates [pubmed_29753897, pubmed_38843960]

### 5. **Perioperative Measures**
- Hospital length of stay [pubmed_35113188, pubmed_29753897]
- Blood loss [pubmed_29753897]
- Complication rates [pubmed_29753897]

### 6. **Quality of Life**
- SF-36 physical component [pubmed_30062947]
- Patient satisfaction [pubmed_30062947]
- Dysphagia assessment [pubmed_30062947]

## Evidence Level
All comparisons derive from **Level 1a evidence** (systematic reviews and meta-analyses of RCTs) across multiple papers [pubmed_31863642, pubmed_40797275, pubmed_29595628, pubmed_40882232, pubmed_29753897, pubmed_28700452, pubmed_31431880, pubmed_35113188, pubmed_30062947, pubmed_38843960].

**Gap:** The papers focus primarily on CDR versus ACDF comparisons; direct three-way comparisons of CDR, CDA, and ACDF using identical outcome metrics are not explicitly presented.
