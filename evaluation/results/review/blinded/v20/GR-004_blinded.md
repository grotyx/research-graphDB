# Outcome Measures Shared with Cervical Disc Replacement (CDR)

Based on the provided papers, **Anterior Cervical Discectomy and Fusion (ACDF)** shares the most outcome measures with cervical disc replacement (CDR/CDA/ACDR), as these interventions are directly compared across multiple studies.

## Shared Outcome Measures (CDR vs ACDF)

The following outcome domains are consistently measured across CDR and ACDF comparisons:

| Outcome Measure | Papers Reporting | Key Findings |
|---|---|---|
| **Neck Disability Index (NDI)** | [pubmed_31863642], [pubmed_29753897], [pubmed_40797275], [pubmed_38843960], [pubmed_35113188], [pubmed_29595628], [pubmed_30062947] | NDI success rates higher in CDR [pubmed_35113188]; SMD 0.41 (95% CI: 0.29-0.52) favoring ACDR [pubmed_29595628]; MD -2.88 (95% CI [-5.93, 0.17], p=0.06) no significant difference [pubmed_31863642] |
| **VAS Neck Pain** | [pubmed_31863642], [pubmed_38843960], [pubmed_31431880] | MD -5.50 (95% CI [-8.49, -2.52], p=0.0003) favoring CDA [pubmed_31863642]; significant improvement across all CDA constraint levels vs ACDF (p<0.05) [pubmed_38843960] |
| **VAS Arm Pain** | [pubmed_31863642], [pubmed_38843960], [pubmed_31431880] | MD -3.78 (95% CI [-7.04, -0.53], p=0.02) favoring CDA [pubmed_31863642]; greatest differential in Unconstrained CDA (p=0.02) [pubmed_38843960] |
| **SF-36 Physical Component Score** | [pubmed_31863642], [pubmed_30062947] | MD 1.93 (95% CI [0.53, 3]) improving with CDA [pubmed_31863642]; TDR showed significant advantages vs ACDF [pubmed_30062947] |
| **Range of Motion (ROM)** | [pubmed_29753897], [pubmed_40797275], [pubmed_38843960] | MD 13.83 degrees (p<0.00001) preserved in ACDR [pubmed_29753897]; WMD 10.37 degrees (p<0.01) in CDA [pubmed_40797275] |
| **Adjacent Segment Disease (ASD)** | [pubmed_29753897], [pubmed_31431880], [pubmed_30062947] | OR 0.27 (p=0.001) reduced with ACDR [pubmed_29753897]; substantial reduction in ASD with TDR [pubmed_30062947] |
| **Complications** | [pubmed_29753897] | OR 0.62 (p=0.006) fewer complications with ACDR [pubmed_29753897] |
| **Reoperation Rates** | [pubmed_29753897] | OR 0.25 (p<0.00001) lower in ACDR [pubmed_29753897] |
| **Neurological Success** | [pubmed_40797275] | OR 1.60 (95% CI: 1.30-1.98, p<0.01) higher in CDA [pubmed_40797275] |
| **Pain Relief (VAS equivalent)** | [pubmed_29753897] | VAS MD -0.50 (p=0.08) equivalent between ACDR and ACDF [pubmed_29753897] |
| **Hospital Stay/Blood Loss** | [pubmed_29753897] | No differences between ACDR and ACDF [pubmed_29753897] |

## Additional Clinical Observations

**Patient Selection Impact:** Pathology type influences outcomes—studies with predominantly **myelopathic cohorts favored ACDF** for decompressive efficacy, while **radiculopathic patients showed more favorable outcomes with CDA** [pubmed_40882232].

**Evidence Gap:** The retrieved papers focus exclusively on **cervical interventions** (CDA/CDR/ACDF). The lumbar intervention (MI-TLIF) [pubmed_39434232] does not share direct comparative outcome measurement with CDR.

# Interventions Sharing Most Outcome Measures with Cervical Disc Replacement (CDR)

Based on the provided papers, **Anterior Cervical Discectomy and Fusion (ACDF)** and **Cervical Disc Arthroplasty (CDA)** are the interventions that share the most outcome measures with CDR across the literature.

## Shared Outcome Domains

### 1. **Functional/Pain Outcomes**
- NDI (Neck Disability Index) [pubmed_29753897, pubmed_31863642, pubmed_30062947]
- VAS (Visual Analog Scale) - neck and upper limb pain [pubmed_31431880, pubmed_30062947]
- Neurological success rates [pubmed_40797275]

### 2. **Motion Preservation**
- Range of motion at operated segment [pubmed_40797275, pubmed_31431880, pubmed_38843960]
- C2-C7 range of motion measurements [pubmed_31431880]

### 3. **Adjacent Segment Outcomes**
- Adjacent segment disease (ASD) [pubmed_31863642, pubmed_29753897, pubmed_30062947, pubmed_38843960]
- Adjacent level surgery rates [pubmed_31863642]
- Adjacent segment degeneration risk [pubmed_38843960]

### 4. **Secondary Surgery Rates**
- Total secondary surgery [pubmed_31863642, pubmed_29753897]
- Secondary surgery at index level [pubmed_31863642]
- Reoperation rates [pubmed_29753897, pubmed_38843960]

### 5. **Perioperative Measures**
- Hospital length of stay [pubmed_35113188, pubmed_29753897]
- Blood loss [pubmed_29753897]
- Complication rates [pubmed_29753897]

### 6. **Quality of Life**
- SF-36 physical component [pubmed_30062947]
- Patient satisfaction [pubmed_30062947]
- Dysphagia assessment [pubmed_30062947]

## Evidence Level
All comparisons derive from **Level 1a evidence** (systematic reviews and meta-analyses of RCTs) across multiple papers [pubmed_31863642, pubmed_40797275, pubmed_29595628, pubmed_40882232, pubmed_29753897, pubmed_28700452, pubmed_31431880, pubmed_35113188, pubmed_30062947, pubmed_38843960].

**Gap:** The papers focus primarily on CDR versus ACDF comparisons; direct three-way comparisons of CDR, CDA, and ACDF using identical outcome metrics are not explicitly presented.
