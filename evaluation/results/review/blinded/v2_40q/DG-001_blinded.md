# Comparative Evidence: TLIF vs PLIF vs OLIF vs ALIF for Lumbar Degenerative Spondylolisthesis

## SAGITTAL ALIGNMENT RESTORATION

### Lordosis Restoration (Meta-analytic Evidence)
**ALIF demonstrates superior segmental lordosis restoration** [pubmed_39575949, Evidence 1a]:
- ALIF provided significantly better restoration of segmental lordosis (SL) compared to TLIF, LLIF, and OLIF
- TLIF conferred significantly lower SL restoration than both ALIF and OLIF
- All comparisons significant at p<0.05

**OLIF superior to PLIF at L5-S1** [pubmed_41313461, Evidence 2b]:
- OLIF demonstrates superior sagittal alignment restoration compared to PLIF at the L5-S1 level

**ALIF superior at L5-S1 in degenerative disease** [pubmed_40066769, Evidence 1a]:
- ALIF demonstrated superior sagittal alignment restoration compared to TLIF at L5-S1

---

## FUSION RATES

**OLIF superior fusion outcomes** [pubmed_39895414, Evidence 1a, Network meta-analysis]:
- OLIF demonstrated significantly higher fusion rates compared to MIS-TLIF (p<0.05)
- Analysis included 22 articles with 1,920 participants
- Large interbody cages via lateral fusion create larger bone grafting area, supporting superior fusion outcomes

---

## COMPLICATION PROFILES

### Clinical Outcomes (Prospective Evidence)

**OLIF vs PLIF for degenerative disease** [pubmed_37950267, Evidence 1a, Meta-analysis of 8 retrospective studies, n=574]:
- OLIF showed significantly better pain relief (VAS −0.43; P=0.04)
- OLIF showed superior functional disability improvement (ODI −1.22; P=0.007)
- OLIF achieved better disc height recovery radiologically

**ALIF vs TLIF at L5-S1** [pubmed_40066769, Evidence 1a]:
- ALIF demonstrated lower complication rates
- ALIF and TLIF showed equivalent long-term safety profiles
- Patient-reported functional outcomes equivalent between techniques

**OLIF vs TLIF for spondylolisthesis** [pubmed_41525955, Evidence 2b, Retrospective cohort]:
- Endo-TLIF excels in minimizing perioperative morbidity through direct visualization
- OLIF-360 provides superior structural reconstruction

**OLIF vs TLIF for lumbar stenosis** [pubmed_40461108, Evidence 2b]:
- OLIF: 4 cases of thigh weakness
- TLIF: 1 case of dural tear
- No serious complications in either group
- Bone fusion obtained in both groups at final follow-up

### Biomechanical Evidence (Finite Element Analysis)

**Distinct complication risk profiles** [pubmed_37224957, Evidence 5, FEA in osteoporotic spine]:
- **TLIF**: Highest hardware stress risk
- **PLIF**: Highest cage subsidence risk
- **ALIF**: Trade-off between disc protection and facet degeneration risk
- (Note: Specific to osteoporotic spine under whole-body vibration)

---

## EVIDENCE GAPS

1. **Direct head-to-head comparisons**: Limited prospective randomized trials directly comparing all four techniques simultaneously
2. **PLIF-specific data**: Minimal direct comparative evidence for PLIF in degenerative spondylolisthesis specifically (most PLIF data comes from comparison with OLIF for general degenerative disease, not spondylolisthesis)
3. **Long-term outcomes**: Most studies report short to medium-term follow-up; long-term spondylolisthesis progression unclear
4. **Patient selection factors**: Limited evidence on which patient subgroups benefit most from each approach
5. **Technique standardization**: Variability in instrumentation, fixation methods, and minimally invasive variations affects comparability

# Comparative Evidence for Lumbar Interbody Fusion Techniques in Degenerative Spondylolisthesis

## 1. FUSION RATES

### ALIF vs PLIF/TLIF with rhBMP-2
[pubmed_25955186] performed a systematic review comparing fusion rates across techniques with and without recombinant human bone morphogenetic protein-2 (rhBMP-2):

- **ALIF with rhBMP-2**: 97.8% vs without rhBMP-2: 88.2% (OR 7.08, p<0.05) [pubmed_25955186]
- **PLIF/TLIF with rhBMP-2**: No significant improvement demonstrated [pubmed_25955186]
- **PLF (posterolateral fusion) with rhBMP-2**: 93.6% vs without rhBMP-2: 83.1% (OR 3.06) [pubmed_25955186]

**Key finding**: ALIF demonstrated the most substantial fusion rate improvement with rhBMP-2 augmentation, whereas PLIF/TLIF showed no significant improvement with this biological adjunct.

---

## 2. SAGITTAL ALIGNMENT RESTORATION (Lordosis)

[pubmed_39575949] conducted a comprehensive meta-analysis across 88 papers with consistent findings:

### Superiority Ranking for Lordosis Restoration:
1. **ALIF: Consistently superior** for lordosis restoration [pubmed_39575949]
2. **TLIF: Consistently inferior** for lordosis restoration [pubmed_39575949]
3. **OLIF vs PLIF**: Mixed results (detailed below)

### OLIF vs PLIF Comparison
[pubmed_37950267] performed the first comprehensive systematic review comparing OLIF and PLIF (8 retrospective case-control studies, 574 patients total; 287 per group):

**Radiological disc height recovery**: OLIF achieved better disc height recovery compared to PLIF [pubmed_37950267]

---

## 3. CLINICAL OUTCOMES (Pain and Functional Disability)

### OLIF vs PLIF [pubmed_37950267]
(8 RCT/retrospective studies, n=574; 287 per group)

| Outcome | OLIF | PLIF | Difference | p-value |
|---------|------|------|-----------|---------|
| **VAS (postoperative improvement)** | −0.43 | Baseline | Significantly better in OLIF | 0.04 |
| **ODI (postoperative improvement)** | −1.22 | Baseline | Significantly better in OLIF | 0.007 |

**Interpretation**: OLIF demonstrated statistically significantly better pain relief (VAS −0.43 points) and functional disability improvement (ODI −1.22 points) compared to PLIF [pubmed_37950267]

### OLIF vs UELIF (Uniportal Endoscopic Lateral Interbody Fusion)
[pubmed_39895414] network meta-analysis (25 articles, 2,245 participants):

- **ODI change**: OLIF demonstrated greater functional improvement compared to UELIF (MD: −1.93, 95% CI: −3.68 to −0.18) [pubmed_39895414]
- **Overall finding**: No significant differences in functional improvement among most procedures [pubmed_39895414]

## 4. COMPLICATION PROFILES

### Overall Complication Rates
[pubmed_23646277] systematized complication data for PLIF and TLIF:

- **Overall complication rate range**: 8–80% with an average of 36.4% [pubmed_23646277]

### Common Complications (PLIF/TLIF) [pubmed_23646277]:
- Subsidence
- Osteolysis
- Postoperative radiculopathy
- Heterotopic ossification
- Dural tear
- Interbody implant migration
- Intraoperative neurologic injury

### Technique-Specific Complications

**OLIF vs TLIF for degenerative spondylolisthesis** [pubmed_38622724]:
- Meta-analysis comparing oblique lateral approach vs transforaminal approach
- Study objective stated but specific quantitative complication data not provided in abstract [pubmed_38622724]

**Endo-TLIF vs OLIF-360** [pubmed_41525955] (Retrospective cohort, degenerative lumbar spondylolisthesis):
- Endo-TLIF: Provides direct decompression under endoscopic visualization
- OLIF-360: Restores disc height and lordosis through indirect decompression
- Relative advantages regarding perioperative outcomes remain uncertain [pubmed_41525955]

## EVIDENCE GAPS

1. **Direct head-to-head comparisons**: Limited RCT data directly comparing all four techniques in the same population [pubmed_39575949, pubmed_37950267]

2. **Complication specificity**: Overall complication rates reported (36.4% average for PLIF/TLIF) but individual technique comparison lacking precise rates [pubmed_23646277]

4. **OLIF fusion rates**: Not reported in comparison studies; OLIF primarily studied for alignment and symptom outcomes [pubmed_37950267]

5. **Minimally invasive variants**: Limited comparative data for endoscopic approaches (Endo-TLIF, UELIF) [pubmed_39895414, pubmed_41525955]

## CLINICAL RECOMMENDATION SYNTHESIS

Based on evidence level 1a systematic reviews and meta-analyses:

- **Best lordosis restoration**: ALIF [pubmed_39575949]
- **Avoid for lordosis**: TLIF (consistently inferior) [pubmed_39575949]
- **Best pain/function outcomes in comparative studies**: OLIF vs PLIF comparison favors OLIF [pubmed_37950267]
- **Best fusion rate augmentation**: ALIF with rhBMP-2 (97.8%) [pubmed_25955186]
